ClipboardManager is a powerful yet simple clipboard history manager designed specifically for Mac users who value efficiency and productivity.

🎯 KEY FEATURES

• Clipboard History
  - Automatically saves your last 20 clipboard items
  - Supports text, images, and files
  - Real-time thumbnail preview for images
  - System icons for files and folders

• Quick Access
  - Press ⌘ + Shift + V to open instantly
  - Click any item to copy it back
  - Menu bar integration for easy access
  - Auto-close after copying

• Smart Organization
  - Pin frequently used items to the top
  - Pinned items stay on top when new content is copied
  - Orange pin icon for easy identification
  - One-tap unpin to restore normal order

• Multiple Operations
  - Tap to copy
  - Swipe left to reveal pin/delete buttons
  - Right-click for context menu
  - Search to filter history

• Beautiful Interface
  - Clean and modern design
  - Smooth animations and transitions
  - Hover and click visual feedback
  - Native macOS look and feel

• Privacy First
  - All data stored locally on your Mac
  - No cloud sync, no data collection
  - No internet connection required
  - Complete privacy control

• Lightweight & Fast
  - Minimal memory footprint (~37MB)
  - Instant search and filtering
  - Smooth scrolling with large history
  - Optimized for Apple Silicon

🎨 PERFECT FOR

- Developers copying code snippets
- Writers managing multiple text drafts
- Designers working with images
- Anyone who copies and pastes frequently

💡 SMART FEATURES

Pin Important Items:
Keep your most-used content at your fingertips. Pinned items stay at the top even when you copy new things.

Swipe Gestures:
Drag items left to reveal pin and delete buttons - just like iOS. Smooth, intuitive, and fast.

Visual Search:
Type to instantly filter your clipboard history. Find that link or text you copied hours ago in seconds.

File Support:
Copy files and folders just like in Finder. ClipboardManager preserves the exact file type and location.

📱 REQUIREMENTS

- macOS 12.0 or later
- 10MB disk space
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac

🔐 PRIVACY

ClipboardManager does not:
- Collect any personal data
- Send data over the internet
- Track your usage
- Show ads

All clipboard history is stored locally on your Mac and never leaves your device.

⚡ GET STARTED

1. Download and install
2. Launch from Applications
3. Press ⌘ + Shift + V
4. Start copying!

The app runs in your menu bar and launches at login (can be disabled in settings).
